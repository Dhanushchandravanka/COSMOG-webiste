About COSMOG


The students of Computer Science and Design (CSG) proudly present COSMOG, a dynamic event crafted to highlight the multifaceted talents of our department. COSMOG combines both technical and non-technical rounds, along with creative challenges, to offer a comprehensive platform for participants to showcase their skills and creativity.

This event is designed not only to test your technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities but also to encourage innovation and artistic expression through various creative rounds. COSMOG aims to expose the strengths and diversity of the CSG department to a wider audience, fostering collaboration and pride among students.
